# ZERO WASTED

**ZERO WASTED** is a hyperlocal, real-time resource redistribution network designed to bridge the gap between surplus and scarcity.

It focuses on *urgency*, *proximity*, and *accountability*—because the problem isn’t a lack of resources, it’s bad coordination.

---

## 🚨 The Problem

### Resource Mismatch
Cities discard massive amounts of usable food and supplies while people nearby struggle to access basic necessities.

### Coordination Gap
Surplus and need exist at the same time, but current systems fail to connect them fast enough.

### System Failure
Most platforms rely on static listings that don’t account for urgency, perishability, or real-time demand.

---

## 💡 The Solution

ZERO WASTED streamlines redistribution into **four simple steps**:

1. **List Surplus**  
   Businesses and individuals list excess food or resources in seconds.

2. **Set Conditions**  
   Time limits, quantities, and pickup constraints ensure clarity and fairness.

3. **Request Items**  
   NGOs and verified individuals submit specific, real-time needs.

4. **Match & Deliver**  
   A smart matching algorithm pairs resources based on urgency and proximity.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### Community Reliability Score
A trust-based accountability system that rewards responsible behavior and discourages misuse.

### Impact Partnerships
Companies can sponsor redistribution efforts and gain visibility into measurable, real-world social impact.

### Verified Rewards
Sponsorship rewards unlock **only after confirmed redistribution**, ensuring authenticity and transparency.

Turn waste into opportunity.

Make cities smarter, fairer, and more human—**in real time**.
